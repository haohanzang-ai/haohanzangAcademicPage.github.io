About

I am a rising junior at Cedar Park High School (Class of 2028) interested in computational biology, agentic AI systems, and plant science. This summer I am conducting research under Prof. Naichen Shi at Northwestern University, and studying plant stress responses at the UT Austin High School Research Academy.

I am a 2× international biology gold medalist (UKBO & UKBC), a 1st-Prize winner at the International Clarinet Association Junior Competition, and a 2× 5A State Band Champion currently serving as Drum Major.
